# Dentist Clinic Chatbot - Features Brainstorm

## Core Features (Already Mentioned)
1. **General Q&A Chat** - Answer patient questions about dental services, procedures, office policies
2. **Check Available Slots** - View available appointment times
3. **Book Appointments** - Schedule appointments through the chatbot

---

## Appointment Management
4. **View Upcoming Appointments** - Patients can see their scheduled appointments
5. **Reschedule Appointments** - Change appointment date/time
6. **Cancel Appointments** - Cancel appointments with optional reason
7. **Appointment History** - View past appointments and treatments
8. **Waitlist Management** - Join waitlist for preferred time slots, get notified when slots open
9. **Recurring Appointments** - Set up regular check-ups (e.g., every 6 months)
10. **Appointment Reminders** - Automated reminders via preferred channel (SMS/Email/Push)

---

## Pre-Appointment Features
11. **Pre-Appointment Forms** - Complete medical history, insurance info, consent forms before visit
12. **Symptom Assessment** - Basic triage questions to help prioritize urgent cases
13. **Preparation Instructions** - Get instructions for specific procedures (e.g., fasting, medications)
14. **Insurance Verification** - Check insurance coverage and benefits before appointment
15. **Service Information** - Learn about services offered, duration, and what to expect

---

## Post-Appointment Features
16. **Post-Appointment Follow-up** - Check-in messages after procedures
17. **Care Instructions** - Receive post-treatment care instructions
18. **Prescription Refills** - Request prescription refills
19. **Treatment Plan Details** - View recommended treatment plans and next steps
20. **Feedback & Reviews** - Submit feedback about the visit

---

## Information & Education
21. **Dentist Profiles** - View dentist bios, specialties, and availability
22. **Service Pricing** - Get estimates for common procedures
23. **Office Hours & Location** - Check hours, get directions, parking information
24. **Dental Care Tips** - Educational content about oral hygiene and dental health
25. **FAQ** - Common questions about procedures, policies, insurance

---

## Emergency & Urgent Care
26. **Emergency Contact** - Quick access to emergency dental services
27. **Urgent Appointment Requests** - Priority booking for dental emergencies
28. **Pain Management Guidance** - Basic guidance for dental pain relief (with disclaimer)

---

## Patient Account Management
29. **Profile Management** - Update contact info, preferences, medical history
30. **Payment History** - View past invoices and payment records
31. **Insurance Information** - Manage insurance details
32. **Family Account Management** - Manage appointments for family members

---

## Notifications & Communication
33. **Multi-channel Notifications** - SMS, Email, Push notifications based on preference
34. **Appointment Confirmations** - Automated confirmations with calendar integration
35. **Weather/Closure Alerts** - Notify about office closures or delays
36. **Promotional Offers** - Receive information about special offers or discounts

---

## Advanced Features
37. **Multi-language Support** - Chat in preferred language
38. **Voice Input/Output** - Voice interaction for accessibility
39. **Photo Upload** - Share photos of dental issues for preliminary assessment
40. **Calendar Integration** - Sync appointments with Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.
41. **Payment Processing** - Pay copays or bills through the chatbot
42. **Referral Management** - Request referrals to specialists
43. **Document Access** - Download receipts, treatment plans, X-ray reports
44. **Accessibility Features** - Screen reader support, high contrast mode, font size adjustment

---

## Analytics & Personalization (Backend)
45. **Smart Recommendations** - Suggest appointment times based on patient history
46. **Preventive Care Reminders** - Personalized reminders based on treatment history
47. **Treatment Compliance** - Track and remind about follow-up treatments

---

## Integration Features
48. **Social Media Integration** - Share positive experiences (optional)
49. **Loyalty Program** - Check points, rewards, referral bonuses
50. **Telehealth Consultation** - Schedule virtual consultations for minor issues

---
